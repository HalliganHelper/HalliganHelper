What is this?

A TA System

Halligan Helper allows Teacher's Assistants to quickly and easily locate and help students. A digital wait list updates in real time, enabling TAs to focus on helping students rather than organizing them.

An Availability System

Gone are the days of arriving in the computer labs, only to find that all of the machines are taken! Halligan Helper keeps track of when computers are being used (in real time, naturally), and allows students to determine if there is space available for them to work.
